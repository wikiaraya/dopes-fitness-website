Payment Policy

At Dopes Fitness, we provide subscription-based fitness services. Please review our payment policy below:

1. Payment Gateway

All payments are securely processed via Razorpay. We do not store your credit/debit card details.

2. Subscription Fee

The subscription fee is clearly mentioned before checkout. By proceeding with payment, you agree to pay the specified amount.

3. Auto-Renewal

Some subscription plans may auto-renew. You will be notified before renewal and can cancel anytime.

4. Failed Transactions

If a transaction fails, please check:

  • Sufficient balance in your account
  • Correct card/payment details entered
  • Network connectivity during the transaction

5. Refunds

Refunds are covered under our Refund Policy.

6. Contact Us

For payment-related queries, email us at dopesfitness@gmail.com.
