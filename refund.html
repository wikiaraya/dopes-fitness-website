Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Dopes Fitness, we aim to provide a smooth and transparent payment process. Please read our Refund & Cancellation policy carefully.

Subscription Payments

  • All subscription payments made through Razorpay are non-refundable once processed.
  • We encourage you to review the subscription details before making a payment.

Cancellations

  • You can cancel your subscription anytime, but the cancellation will be effective from the next billing cycle.
  • No refunds will be issued for the current billing cycle.

Refund Exceptions

  • A refund will only be provided if there was an error in payment processing (such as double-charging).
  • In such cases, refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days.
